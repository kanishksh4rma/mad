Exposure & Ritual Prevention for OCD


Compulsions/Rituals


    

  • Goal is to demonstrate that rituals are short-term
anxiety reducers that ultimately increase
obsessions
    • 

  • RITUAL PREVENTION is the key intervention
    • 



Success Rate %


    

  • Exposure and response prevention (ERP) appears to be the most
effective treatment currently available, with 50-60% recovered
    • 

  • Typical OCD symptom reduction: Medication 40-60%, CBT 60-80%
    • 

  • Up to 25% of patients REFUSE or DO NOT COMPLETE Exposure
    •

  • Items are ranked in order from least distressing

  • Serves as the backbone of the exposures
